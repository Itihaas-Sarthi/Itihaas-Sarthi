About the Robot

Itihaas Sarthi is an autonomous guiding robot built from the ground up to preserve, narrate, and bring Indian heritage to life for every visitor.

What Is It

Built for India's heritage

Itihaas Sarthi combines autonomous navigation, GPS-powered location awareness, multilingual audio narration, and accessibility-first design into a single robot — purpose-built to guide visitors through India's most significant cultural landmarks.

01Accessibility — A retractable handle assists visually impaired visitors, guiding them safely with vibration alerts.
02Cultural Narration — Speaks curated historical facts about each site in the visitor's chosen language.
03Autonomous Navigation — Moves safely using ultrasonic sensors, cliff detectors, and a camera to avoid obstacles.
04Practical Design — Includes a storage compartment for visitor belongings and a durable weather-resistant chassis.
