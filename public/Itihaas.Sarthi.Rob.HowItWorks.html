How It Works

From a single scan to a full narrated tour — five steps that power the entire Itihaas Sarthi experience.

The Process

From activation to narration

01
RFID Activation The robot wakes up when an authorised RFID tag is scanned. This ensures only approved users can activate the system, keeping it secure and ready to assist.
02
Location Detection GPS pinpoints the robot's exact position. The onboard camera cross-references the surroundings to identify the heritage site and load the correct content.
03
Facts Retrieved Historical facts are pulled from preloaded onboard memory — no internet required. Content is curated, accurate, and tailored to the specific monument or site.
04
Audio Narration A built-in text-to-speech module reads the facts aloud through the speaker. Visitors can choose their preferred language using the language selector button, and adjust volume with tactile controls.
05
Guided Movement Ultrasonic sensors detect obstacles and people. Cliff sensors prevent falls at steps or edges. Vibration motors alert the user before turns or stops — keeping every visitor safe throughout the tour.
