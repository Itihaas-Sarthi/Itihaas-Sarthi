Features

Everything Itihaas Sarthi can do — built for all visitors, designed for India's toughest heritage environments.

What It Offers

Built for everyone, everywhere

Accessibility First A retractable handle extends automatically for visually impaired visitors. Vibration alerts signal turns, obstacles, and stops — making heritage sites accessible to everyone.
Multilingual Narration Speaks cultural facts in Hindi, English, and regional languages via a simple selector button — so every visitor connects with history in their own tongue.
GPS-Powered Context Knows exactly where it is and automatically loads the right historical facts — no manual input needed from the visitor at any point.
Camera Vision An onboard camera identifies monuments in real time, helping the robot understand its surroundings and enrich narration with visual context.
Storage Compartment Carries visitor essentials — water bottles, bags, and belongings — so tourists can explore hands-free and comfortably throughout the site.
Safe & Reliable Power A rechargeable 7.4V Li-ion battery with a built-in management system prevents overcharging and keeps the robot running safely all day long.
